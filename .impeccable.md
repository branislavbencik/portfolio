## Design Context

### Users

Design Lead / Head of Design evaluating a Senior or generalist Product Designer hire. Design-literate, impatient with fluff, immediately recognizes craft. Target employers: FTMO, Nebius, n8n, Make, Productboard, Apify, JetBrains. Secondary audience: recruiter-as-filter.

Full audience + JTBD in `positioning.md` → §Voice.

### Brand Personality

**Precise · Deliberate · Personal.** (Updated 2026-04-22 from v2's "Precise · Deliberate · Dense / Coldly Calculated.")

The generalist frame is human and honest, not cold. "I happen to be useful" is the Branislav register — warmer than a brand voice, more honest than polished marketing prose.

### Aesthetic Direction

**Editorial Light Technical.** Off-white canvas, zinc palette, sharp corners, hard 1px structure — but **no full-card frames**. Bare thumbnails + text below, marijanapav.com register. Devtool-precise, not dashboard-decorated.

Primary reference: **marijanapav.com** (bare-frame editorial cards). Carryover references: Vercel, Linear, Warp (typographic precision, monochrome restraint).

Anti-references, theme notes, link-color two-tier system, and visual constraints: `positioning.md` → §Aesthetic direction + §Visual constraints.

Execution tokens (colors, typography scale, spacing, motion, component architecture): `docs/DESIGN.md`.

### Design Principles

1. The interface is the portfolio.
2. Density over decoration.
3. Functional quirks only.
4. Direct copy, no marketing voice.
5. Trust the system (`positioning.md` → `docs/DESIGN.md` → `globals.css`).

Expanded rationale in `positioning.md` → §Design principles.
